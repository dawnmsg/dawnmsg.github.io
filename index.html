About Me

I am a Senior Research Software Engineer at Microsoft Research Asia, specializing in artificial intelligence, large language models, natural language processing, and agent systems. I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from Tsinghua University (2019) and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Beijing Normal University (2016).

My work primarily focuses on developing next-generation LLM-powered agent systems. This includes enhancing reasoning, planning, and task-solving capabilities to improve AI's effectiveness and generalizability in real-world applications. You can learn more about me on LinkedIn. You can also explore more of my publications on Google Scholar and the ACL Anthology.

I served as a volunteer reviewer for top-tier conferences and journals, including NeurIPS, ICLR, ACL ARR (ACL, EMNLP, NAACL), ICASSP, INTERSPEECH, etc.

Beyond my professional work, I have a wide range of interests. You can explore more about my daily life in the gallery tab.

Contact me: Shaoguang.Mao@microsoft.com, dawnmsg941107@gmail.com
